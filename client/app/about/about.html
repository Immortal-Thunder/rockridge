Mission

Rockridge is an open source software project that aims to demystify the process of building a personal financial plan.
We aim to empower users to take control of their financial futures and educate them along the way.

Details

Rockridge is a simple, clean, and intuitive web application that prompts the user to input all the neccessary information needed to build a plan.
After the information is entered, complex calculations are done behind the scenes to create the best plan for that particular user.
These calculations are based on years of combined experience and research of our team, and are reliable and accurate.*

Drive

Why did we create Rockridge? We saw first hand how broken the personal financial advising industry is.
Many individuals are weary of financial advisors - and for good reason.
Rockridge is a safe, zero pressure environment guides a user along the path to financial freedom and independence.
Whether you are trying to pay down debt, save for retirement, analyse your personal risk,
or just understand your financial situation better - Rockridge can help you achieve your financial goals.
Rockridge doesn't cost you anything, but it can save you a lifetime of worry and stress.
